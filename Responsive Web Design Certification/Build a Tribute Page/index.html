Amitabh Bachchan

Brief timeline of Amitabh Bachchan's life

  • 1942: Born on 11 October, 1942 at Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, India to Sri Harivansh Rai Bachchan and Teji Bachchan.
  • 1969: Early Career: Mr. Bachchan made his film debut in 1969 with 'Saat Hindustani'. He got a supporting role in 'Anand' alongside then superstar Rajesh Khanna.
  • 1973: Angry Young Man: 'The Angry Young Man' took birth with movies like 'Zanjeer', 'Deewar' and 'Sholay' with Salim-Javed. This was the year when Amitabh Bachchan married Jaya Bhaduri.
    He also appeared in many other successful movies like 'Abhimaan', 'Namak Haraam', 'Trishul', 'Shakti' and 'Roti Kapda Aur Makaan'.
  • 1975+: Superstardom: 'Kabhi Kabhi', 'Amar Akbar Anthony', 'Parvarish', 'Don', 'Muqaddar Ka Sikandar', 'Satte Pe Satta', 'Dostana', 'Coolie', 'Naseeb', 'Lawaaris', 'Kaalia', 'Yaarana' and many many more were among some of the most successful movies of that decade.
  • 1988+: Politics: He unsuccessfully tried his three year stint in politics but came back to movies with 'Shahenshah'.
  • 1996+: Business: Amitji had some not-so-successful year of his career when he incorporated ABCL (Amitabh Bachchan Corporation, Ltd.). ABCL didn't do well and he lost millions which eventually led to its operations collapse in 1997.
  • 2000+: Return to prominence: Amitabh Bachachan came back as a Superstar with movies like 'Mohabbatein', 'Baghban', 'Bunty Aur Babli', 'Sarkar', 'Cheeni Kum' and 'Piku'. 'Kaun Banega Crorepati' TV serial also became massive hit TV serial in Indian televison history which has run for more than six seasons.
  • 2020: COVID: Amitabh Bachachan tested COVID positive and was hospitalized for treatment. He returned home safely after getting treated. Thank God!
